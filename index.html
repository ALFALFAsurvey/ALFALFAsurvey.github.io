ALFALFA Survey Legacy Homepage

The Arecibo Legacy Fast ALFA Survey was a state of the art 21~cm neutral hydrogen survey, that observed over a fifth of the sky and detected over 30,000 extragalactic sources.

This website is dedicated to preserving the scientific products of that survey.

ALFALFA Survey Websites will soon be hosted with GitHub Pages.
