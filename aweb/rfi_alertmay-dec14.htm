RFI alert for May, June, Sep, Dec 2014

This is an update to the released alert with schedule change for the June 2014 dates.

We have time on the evening of June 12, but should be ok.

We will have to check later when we know about possible fall LBW time.

Subject: Re: GPS-L3 (1381 MHz)  RFI Alert: ***Schedule change***
Date: 2014-05-07 15:00
From: Dan Mertely 

We have received updated notice from the US Space Command (USSC),
Colorado Springs, CO, informing the RA community of a change
in the schedule for the June, 2014 on-orbit checkout of SV67
(GPS 2F-6).  It appears that their test date has been pulled-up
by 1 day.  The observatories should expect increased RFI @ 1381
MHz during the dates & times indicated below.  The GPS 2F-6 launch
is currently scheduled for 20140515.

(These extended-duration tests are in addition to the regular,
daily short-duration (< 180 sec/day) GPS-L3 tests allowed for in
the USAF/NSF MOU.)

VLA & VLBA schedulers should attempt to schedule 1381 MHz use
around these times if possible.  Observing scientists should
be made aware of the increased possibility of corrupted data
on these dates and times.  VLA and VLBA operators should note
unusual L-band Tsys or total power fluctuations observed during
these times in the Operator Log forwarded to observers.

Please contact me for coordination should any of these dates
and times conflict with planned radio astronomy observations.

-Mert
